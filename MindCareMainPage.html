Everything You Need to Thrive

From an empathetic AI chatbot to personalized coping strategies, Mitr is packed with features to support you.

Core Features

AI-Powered Chatbot

Get empathetic 24/7 support. Our chatbot uses sentiment analysis to provide responses tailored to your well-being.

Mood Tracking

Log your mood daily and visualize trends over time, empowering you to self-reflect and manage your mental health.

SOS Emergency Trigger

Detects keywords of distress and immediately provides helpline numbers, ensuring you get help when you need it most.

Anonymous Peer Forum

A safe, moderated community space where you can connect with other students without fear of judgment.

Confidential Booking System

For appointment with on-campus counsellor or mental health helpline.

Dashboard

A "Mental Wellness Report" showing mood trends, stress factors, and engagement with peers.

Personalized Coping Strategies

AI suggests custom stress-relief activities like breathing or journaling based on your mood history.

Mindfulness & Focus Tools

Built-in guided meditation, focus music, and Pomodoro timers to help you stay calm and productive.

Customizable Digital Pet

A virtual pet that grows healthier as you engage, visualizing your personal progress in a fun way.
